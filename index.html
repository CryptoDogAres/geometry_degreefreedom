Geometry Degree of Freedom

This site hosts notes about a polygon ratio problem and its modern proof workflows.

Notebook summaries

  1. Polygon Ratio Problem - A collected set of geometric proofs for the classic ratio identity, plus a discussion of degrees of freedom and a one-parameter generalization using an ellipse model.
  2. SymPy Wu and Groebner polynomial reduction - A coordinate-geometry encoding in SymPy that constructs the configuration and algebraically the ratio.
  3. Newclid Methods - An overview of modern automated geometry provers with a Newclid-driven proof attempt, highlighting how auxiliary constructions and symbolic deduction are used in practice.
